About Me

My photography journey began in my second last year of universtiy. I began photographing abandoned areas in Windsor such as abandoned train tracks, buildings, and houses. These areas were left untouched for years, causing wild life to reclaim the land. I was fascinated at how ecosystems can repair themselves after being left alone. As you can see in the descriptions of my collections, my photography harbours on environmentalism as I bring attention to vast lands being unprooted for development, and water that is unlivable. Many of these areas were once family homes, now left abandoned. I enjoy different uses of colours and mediums to capture the natural beauty of nature and the world around us! I hope you enjoy browsing my online gallery as much as I enjoyed taking these photos.
